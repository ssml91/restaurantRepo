About Us

The philosophy of HAMA SUSHI, which pursues something delicious, is that the owner continues to make food even if he makes a lot of money. Second, even if he makes a lot of money, the owner chooses the ingredients himself. Third, even if he makes a lot of money, he doesn't increase the number of stores much. Fourth, even if he makes a lot of money, he doesn't touch anything other than his field. HAMA SUSHI does it to keep the best to taste the food. Since 2020.
