WHERE TO START

The Basics

HTML

We set up our pages using HTML5, using the most current doctype, and set up to be built responsively.

Interactivity

The interactivity of our components are built with Javascript and jQuery. jQuery can be applied with the latest CDN. Add the script tags to your pages and see your site come to life. 

    
         <!--  THIS IS  THE STANDARD HTML5 DOCTYPE -->
        <html lang="en">
        <head>
            <metacharset="UTF-8">
            <metaname="viewport;" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">

            <!-- THIS IS THE FONT AWESOME CDN -->
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/
            libs/font-awesome/5.13.0/css/all.min.css">

            <!-- THIS IS THE LINK TO OUR CUSTOM STYLES -->
            <link rel="stylesheet" href="css/styles.css">

            <!-- THIS IS THE LINK TO jQUERY -->
            <script src="https://code.jquery.com/jquery-3.5.1.min.js"
                integrity="sha256-9/aliU8dGd2tb6OSsuzixeV4y/faTqgFtohetphbbj0=" 
                crossorigin="anonymous"><script>

            <!-- THE GOOGLE FONTS CDN CAN BE FOUND IN styles.scss -->

            <title>Starting Template</title>
        </head>
        <body>
            
        </body>
        <html>

The Rest of the Components

Grids

We have a responsive grid system that is class based. You will find it easy to use and its uses are limitless. You can apply it to menus, forms, and so much more.


Forms

With any business, your online presence will at least a form or two. We've created forms to support your restaurant needs. Checkout our options for gift cards, processing online orders, or becoming a member.


Tables

Tables are yet another option for you to display your information to your guests. We've seen our customers use them for an assortment of ways, from a locations table, to a glossary to describe their sushi rolls. Our table can be searched, sorted, and you can bring your data in from an API.


Styles and Layouts

We have a selection of fonts and icons that we recommend for our pages, but you can choose to use as many or as little as your prefer.

We like to use GoogleFonts because they have a large selection, you wil be able to find almost any style to suit your needs. We like to use FontAwesome for our icons. Like GoogleFonts, they have a huge selection and are easy to use. Both GoogleFonts and FontAwesome are available through CDN.
