Tables

Tables can be used in a variety of ways in your restaurant website. Use them for displaying locations, menu descriptions, listing coupons or promos, and even customer reviews!


Our tables come with a built in search, sort, and are responsive. You can use our table to display information from an API or enter your information locally.
