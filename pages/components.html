Tables

Tables can be used in a variety of ways in your restaurant or cafe website. Use them for displaying locations, menu descriptions, listing coupons or promos, and even customer reviews! Tables have a variety of uses throughout your site.


Our tables come with a built in search function, sort function, and are responsive at any view. You can use our table to display information from an API or enter your information locally. The possibilities are endless!
