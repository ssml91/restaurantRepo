Buttons


Get your customers attention and give them a call-to-action.

Buttons are great to get your customer to complete a call-to-action. Whether it's contacting you for catering or placing an order, buttons will be a great way to get your customers to complete your desired CTA. There are a variety to choose from including different sizes and states.






                <div class="buttonsOnStyles">
                    <button class="btnStyle">BUTTON</button>
                    <button class="hoverBtnStyle">HOVER BUTTON</button>
                    <button class="activeBtnStyle">ACTIVE BUTTON</button>
                </div>

  • Body Copy

    • Paragraphs are styled with a standardized font of .8ems.

  • Transformation to uppercase or lowercase

    Easily transform large bodies of text lower or uppercase by adding a class "upperCase" or "lowerCase" to your element.

    <p class="upperCase"> </p>

    <p class="lowerCase"> </p>

No Print Class

Easily exclude segments of your webpage for printing, by adding a 'no-print' class on any element needed and define it in your css as follows:


                    
    @media print
    {    
        .no-print, .no-print *
        {
            display: none !important;
        }

Icons

Using icon fonts is a great way to add not only style but great visual indicators on your website. We like to use FontAwesome as our resource. Simply add the CDN to the <head></head> section of your webpage and add the icon font tags ><i> to your elements. Here are are few that we recommend.

                    
    <head>
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/
    font-awesome/5.13.0/css/all.min.css">
    </head>
